The Challenge

The Vibe Coding Challenge invites you to develop your own browser game with the help of AI. Explore creatively how large our CO‚ÇÇ footprint is ‚Äì and how we can reduce or even neutralize it.

What is "Vibe Coding"?

"Vibe Coding" is a creative approach to software development where you use the power of AI tools to brainstorm, prototype, and build digital ideas ‚Äì quickly, intuitively, and with a good dose of fun. It's about bringing your vision to life with AI as your co-creator.

Your Mission

Ready to Share Your Game?

We're excited to see what you create! Submit your solution and contribute to a growing library of impactful games.

How to Submit:

You can send your solution via email to info@deckeins.de with your game attached, or follow these steps on GitHub:

  1. Fork this repository: Click here to fork.
  2. Create a folder: Under /submissions/ with your name (e.g., /submissions/your-name/).
  3. Upload your solution: Add your HTML file or full project folder to your new directory. Don't forget a screenshot.png!
  4. Open a pull request: We'll review and merge your awesome creation!
